About PasaHero

Our mission is to make travel in the Philippines accessible to everyone

Our Story

Who We Are

PasaHero was founded in 2015 with one simple goal: to provide the cheapest travel deals in the Philippines without compromising quality.

What started as a small travel blog sharing budget tips has grown into the country's leading budget travel agency. Our team of travel experts negotiates directly with hotels, airlines, and tour operators to secure exclusive discounts we pass on to you.

We believe that everyone deserves to experience the beauty of the Philippines, regardless of budget. Our name "PasaHero" reflects our commitment to being your travel hero, helping you discover amazing destinations at prices you can afford.
